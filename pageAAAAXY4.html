AAAAXY Hint #3

If you have gone down a single path, then you have one item. If you have gone down 2, you can beat the game. If you have gone down 3, your set. If you have not found the anti-hub, keep looking. You need to go there.

Cleats: Ride the platform up untill you get to the shelf with the arrow pointing to the green room. Follow the arrow to get to the Anti-Hub, or go to the blue room to see the hub again.

Coil: Push the platform untill it hits the button, then step up the blue blocks. Then push those platforms to your pleasure. Go down the path with the diagram of a loaded truck to go to the Anti-Hub, or go the other way to get back to the hub. I would advise using this path to go to the Anti-Hub without grabbing the Gloves first. This path is longer than specified, and the dark blue room essentially requires cheese that uses the gloves, or pain.
Gloves: Push the button by grabbing the platform and climb the blue blocks to get up. Use your grabby powers to proceed, then in the next room jump on the platforms to go to the Anti-Hub, or go straight to go back to the hub.
